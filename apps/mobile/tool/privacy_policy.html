Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 18, 2026

BabyMon ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your child. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the BabyMon mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your name (optional)
  • Your date of birth (for age verification)
  • Authentication credentials (securely hashed)

1.2 Baby Profile Information

When you create a baby profile, we collect:

  • Baby's name
  • Date of birth or due date
  • Gestational age at birth
  • Biological sex (optional)

1.3 Health & Development Data

As you use the App, you may voluntarily record:

  • Feeding logs (breastfeeding, formula, solids)
  • Sleep logs (duration, quality)
  • Health records (temperature, symptoms, medications)
  • Growth measurements (weight, height, head circumference)
  • Developmental milestones
  • Allergies and medical conditions
  • Vaccination records
  • Journal entries and notes

1.4 Photos

If you use the baby album feature, we store photos you upload. Photos are stored securely in cloud storage (AWS S3).

1.5 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage analytics (features used, session duration)
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • IP address (for security and fraud prevention)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality
  • Generate personalized developmental insights and content
  • Power the AI Companion (see Section 7)
  • Process payments through Stripe
  • Send push notifications (with your consent)
  • Improve the App through analytics and crash reporting
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only:

  • With service providers: Stripe (payments), Firebase (notifications), AWS (storage), Neon (database hosting). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • With medical team members: If you explicitly invite a partner, family member, or healthcare provider to your baby's care team, they can view the data you choose to share.
  • As required by law: If we receive a valid legal request.
  • With your consent: For any other purpose you authorize.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Secure authentication with hashed passwords (bcrypt)
  • JWT-based session management with refresh token rotation
  • Regular security audits and dependency updates
  • Rate limiting and DDoS protection

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time through the App's Settings screen or by contacting us at support@babymon.app. Deletion is completed within 30 days. Some anonymized or aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

BabyMon is designed for use by parents and guardians to track their children's development. The App is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without parental consent, please contact us immediately.

7. AI Companion & On-Device Processing

The AI Companion feature uses on-device machine learning (via llamadart) to generate parenting guidance and answer questions. Key privacy features:

  • On-device inference: AI processing happens entirely on your phone. No child health data is sent to external AI services (OpenAI, Google, etc.).
  • No human review: AI responses are generated automatically without human review of your specific queries.
  • Local context: The AI uses your baby's age, stage, and profile data — but this data never leaves your device during AI inference.
  • Medical disclaimer: The AI Companion is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@babymon.app. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services. Please review their privacy policies:

  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Firebase: Push notifications and analytics
  • AWS (Amazon Web Services): Cloud storage and infrastructure
  • Neon: Database hosting

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable, to protect your data during international transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App or via email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices:

  • Email: support@babymon.app
  • Website: babymon.app
