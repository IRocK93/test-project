## Full Description (Google Play / App Store)

BabyMon is your intelligent parenting companion, designed to help you navigate the journey from pregnancy through toddlerhood with confidence and clarity.

### CORE FEATURES

**📊 Comprehensive Tracking**
Track feeding (breastfeeding, formula, solids), sleep patterns, health records, growth measurements, and developmental milestones — all in one beautifully designed app.

**🧠 AI Companion (On-Device)**
Get evidence-informed parenting guidance powered by on-device AI. Ask questions about your baby's development, sleep, feeding, and health. All AI processing happens entirely on your phone — your data never leaves your device.

**📈 Developmental Stage System**
Follow your baby's journey week-by-week from pregnancy through age two. Each stage brings personalized content, milestone expectations, and expert advice cards tailored to your child's exact age.

**🎯 Milestone Tracking**
Track developmental milestones across five domains: gross motor, fine motor, language & communication, cognitive, and social-emotional. Celebrate achievements and know when to seek guidance.

**👥 Care Team Sharing**
Invite partners, family members, and healthcare providers to your baby's care team. Share selected data and collaborate on care.

**📸 Baby Album**
Capture and organize precious moments in a beautiful photo album. Photos are securely stored in the cloud.

**🔒 Privacy-First Design**
We take your family's privacy seriously. The AI Companion runs entirely on-device. Health data is encrypted in transit and at rest. You control what data is shared and with whom.
